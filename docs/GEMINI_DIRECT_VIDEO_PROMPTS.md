## ðŸ’¡ Tips for Best Results

1. **Keep it simple** - Gemini works best with clear, straightforward descriptions
2. **Specify duration** - Mention "5 seconds" or "quick 3-second clip"
3. **One main action** - Focus on one key movement or event per clip
4. **Lighting matters** - Mention "warm lighting" or "blue screen glow"
5. **Style consistency** - Use similar descriptors across clips ("cinematic", "modern", "professional")
6. **Avoid complex scenes** - Multiple characters or intricate actions may not work well
