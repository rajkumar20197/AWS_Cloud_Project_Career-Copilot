### Sizes:

- **Favicon:** 16x16, 32x32, 48x48
- **Mobile:** 60x60, 120x120
- **Desktop:** 200x200, 400x400
- **Print:** Vector (SVG) for any size

- Minimum clear space: 1/4 of logo height
- Never stretch or distort
- Maintain aspect ratio

### Colors:

- **Primary:** Full color gradient
- **Dark mode:** White outline version
- **Monochrome:** Navy blue only
- **Reversed:** White on dark background
